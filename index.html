GOOD UNIVERSE

We envision a future where menstrual hygiene is prioritized, ensuring that women and girls in marginalized communities have access to necessary resources and support. Addressing menstrual hygiene needs not only promotes their health and dignity but also empowers them to participate fully in society. By advocating for and providing adequate menstrual hygiene management, we aim to eliminate barriers that hinder their well-being and potential.
