About us

Our Story

Welcome to our initiative dedicated to addressing the interconnected issues of climate change, gender inequalities, and detrimental health practices through sustainable menstrual products. Our journey began with a shared commitment to creating a positive impact in both urban and rural communities. We believe that every small change can lead to significant strides toward a healthier planet and a more equitable society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide eco-friendly, plastic-free menstrual products that empower women and promote environmental sustainability. By choosing our products, you are not only taking a step towards better personal health but also contributing to the global fight against plastic pollution and supporting gender equality.

Our Values

Sustainability: We are committed to offering products that are free from plastics and harmful chemicals, ensuring they are safe for both the user and the environment. Empowerment: We aim to empower women through access to safe and sustainable menstrual products, promoting dignity and independence. Community: Our approach is collaborative and participatory, engaging various social strata to address climate change, gender inequalities, and health practices holistically.

Our Approach

We adopt a participatory and collaborative methodology to ensure our initiatives are inclusive and impactful. Our action-driven goals include: Education and Awareness: Conducting workshops and awareness campaigns about sustainable menstruation and its benefits for health and the environment. Community Engagement: Working with local communities to understand their needs and challenges, ensuring our solutions are tailored and effective. Partnerships: Collaborating with like-minded organizations and stakeholders to amplify our impact and reach more communities.
